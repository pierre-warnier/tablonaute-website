Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my progress?

Go to Settings in the app and tap 'Reset Progress' to clear all learning data for your profile.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual profiles in the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Tablonaute works completely offline. No internet connection is required.

What languages are supported?

The app supports 14 languages: English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Dutch, Portuguese, Russian, Polish, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, and Hindi.

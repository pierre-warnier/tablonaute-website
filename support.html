Frequently Asked Questions

Gameplay

What counts as 'mastered'?

A multiplication is mastered when you answer it correctly 3 times in a row. Mastered facts appear white in the progress grid.

How many trophies are there?

There are 27 trophies across 6 categories: Learn, Time Attack, Reverse, Missing Number, Mastery, and General achievements.

How does Time Attack work?

You have 6 seconds to answer each question. Answer correctly to keep your streak going. Your best score is saved.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! Tablonaute works completely offline. No internet connection is required.

What languages are supported?

The app supports 15 languages: English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Dutch, Portuguese, Russian, Ukrainian, Polish, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, and Hindi.

Profiles & Data

How do I reset my progress?

Tap 'Reset Progress' at the bottom of the Home Screen to clear all learning data for your profile.

How do I delete a profile?

Long-press on a profile name on the profile selection screen to delete it and all its data.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual profiles in the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data.

Voice Recognition

Voice recognition isn't working. What should I do?

Make sure you've granted microphone permission to the app. Check that your device language matches the app language. Speak clearly and wait for the microphone icon before answering.

Can I say numbers in words?

Yes! You can say 'forty-two' or '42' - the app understands both spoken words and digits in all supported languages.

Privacy & Safety

Is the app safe for children?

Absolutely! Tablonaute is designed specifically for children. There are no ads, no in-app purchases, no external links during gameplay, and no way to contact strangers. It's a safe, focused learning environment.

Is my data private?

Yes. Tablonaute is privacy-first: all data stays on your device, nothing is sent to the internet, there's no tracking or analytics, and no account is required. You're in complete control.

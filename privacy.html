Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Overview

Tablonaute is developed by Tablonaute Studios powered by Warnier Advisory. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect (or rather, don't collect) and how we handle it.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. Tablonaute is designed to work entirely offline.

  • Does NOT require an internet connection
  • Does NOT collect personal information
  • Does NOT track your location
  • Does NOT use analytics or tracking
  • Does NOT contain advertisements
  • Does NOT share data with third parties

Data Storage

All data (profiles, progress, achievements) is stored locally on your device only. It never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Voice Recognition

The app uses your device's built-in speech recognition. Voice data is processed by your device's operating system. Tablonaute does not record, store, or transmit any voice data.

Children's Privacy

Tablonaute is designed for children and complies with COPPA. We do not collect any information from children. No account creation is required.

Data Deletion

You can delete profiles within the app, reset progress, or uninstall to remove all data.
